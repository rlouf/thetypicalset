The [Deep Image Prior](https://arxiv.org/abs/1711.10925) paper shows that $\mathcal{N}(0, \sigma)$ on the weights + [[convolutional_neural_networks|CNN architecture]] gives a good prior in functional space.

[Residual Pathway Priors for Soft Equivariance Constraints](https://arxiv.org/abs/2112.01388) shows a method to add inductive bias to BNNs with CNN architecture.
