[[economical_systems_tend_to_become_more_complex_as_we_remove_constraints|Economical systems tend to become more complex as we remove constraints]], at the beginning they are fairly simple and we may be able to build models. Then we can move forward in time when new regulation shows up, show data before/after & try to model, etc, etc. It would make a great textbook, and *it would inform policy a lot better than the current mess*.
