```
\begin{equation*}
 \pi_{X}(X) = \frac{1}{\pi\,\gamma \left[1 + \left(\frac{x}{\gamma}\right)^{2}\right]}
\end{equation*}
```
with $\gamma > 0$ the *scale* parameter.

It can be written as a Normal-InverseGamma Mixture.
