## I - Types of money

p13 - In a functional financial system, fiat, deposits, reserves & treasuries are convertible into each other.

p15 - Only Fed can create reserves. In open market operations (OMO) the Fed use reserves to buys treasuries in reserves. When it buys treasuries from non-banks, it needs a bank to mediate the transaction. Commercial banks can convert reserves -> fiat but this is negligible. [[reserve_is_cash|Reserve is cash]].

p17 - Banks settle payments with reserves. p20 - Banks create deposits out of thin sur when make a loan. In volume, deposits >> reserves. Banks can borrow reserves from other banks on the Federal Funds market. [[bank_deposits_are_a_cash_abstraction|Bank deposits are a cash abstraction]]

## III - Shadow banks

p55 - dealers, money market funds, ETF, investment funds and securitization vehicles > Pension funds take exceedingly risky behavior because it's a scam [[la_retraite_par_capitalisation_a_les_memes_problemes_que_la_retraite_par_repartition|La retraite par capitalisation a les mêmes problèmes que la retraite par répartition]]

p56 - Primary dealers are the only ones from whom Fed buys treasuries. p58 - They do matched book repo trades. Borrow repo loans. Depend on overnight loans. Bear Stearns died from banks not wanting to renew their loans. p60 - Repo rates incident in Sept. 2019, Fed stepped in and bailed them out.
