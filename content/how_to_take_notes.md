I used to have unfinished blog posts everywhere because these small bits of information did not justify a full blog post. A blog *post* is too ambitious, framing it as notes is already a lot better.

It is important to think about *what qualifies as a note and what is part of a bigger project*. There is stuff I know about, stuff I want to learn about, and there are larger projects that are more research-y, like the [[blog/notes-bac-ii|Les notes du bac II]] or even part of the [[chess_openings|Chess openings]] stuff. I can do everything in org-roam thanks to org-babel but is it really a good thing? I am not sure yet.
