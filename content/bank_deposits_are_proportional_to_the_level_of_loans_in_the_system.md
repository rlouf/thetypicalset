Bank deposits are created in only two instances:

1.  When cash is deposited at the bank;
2.  When a bank issues a loan.

The loan has to be paid back with deposits; so to repay a loan another loan needs to be made somewhere. [[debt_is_what_sets_the_world_in_motion|Debt is what sets the world in motion]].
