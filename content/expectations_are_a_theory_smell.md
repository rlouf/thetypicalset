I think Friedman cooked this up to explain why [[the_cpi_increases_faster_since_the_70s|the CPI increases faster since the 70s]]. But bringing information from the future into the present is problematic theoretically speaking. We can *project* inflation based on what we currently know but certainly cannot use information from the futue in our models. This can lead to [these absurdities](https://www.econlib.org/archives/2014/03/did_the_rate_in.html).
