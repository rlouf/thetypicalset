MCX - lessons learned

I am writing this after a couple of people directly asked me to. I don't think I have any universal lesson to teach to anyone, however I can tell you my story and hopefully it will apply to yours too.

  • Work in the open
  • Laser-focus on one thing
  • Bring crazy ideas to their conclusion
  • Trust your insticts
  • Know to recognize projects that are greater than you
  • Learn to give up
