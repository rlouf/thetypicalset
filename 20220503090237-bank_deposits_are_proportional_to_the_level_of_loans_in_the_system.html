Bank deposits are proportional to the level of loans in the system

Bank deposits are created in only two instances:

  1. When cash is deposited at the bank;
  2. When a bank issues a loan.

The loan has to be paid back with deposits; so to repay a loan another loan needs to be made somewhere. Debt is what sets the world in motion. And In the end the State holds the financial system together
