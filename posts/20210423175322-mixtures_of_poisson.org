#+title: Mixtures of Poisson
#+filetags: :inprogress:public:

- Negative Binomial: $X \sim Poisson(\lambda)$ with $\lambda \sim Gamma$
- Neyman distribution: $X \sim Poisson(L\theta)$ with $L \sim Poisson$
- Polya-Aeppli: $X = \sum_i^Z Y_i$ where $Z \sim Poisson(\lambda)$ and $Y_i \sim Geometric$
- Geometric: $X \sim Poisson(\lambda)$ with $\lambda \sim Exponential$
- Negative Binomial: $X \sim Poisson(\lambda)$ with $\lambda = \sum_i Poisson(Y_i)$
