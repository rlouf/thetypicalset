#+title: Book reviews and literature notes are different things
#+filetags: :public:
#+LAST_MODIFIED: [2022-01-10 Mon 10:50]

/Literature notes/ are written with the goal in mind to add bits of knowledge to our existing network of thoughts.

/Book review/ is to tell others (maybe you in the future?) what this book is about.
