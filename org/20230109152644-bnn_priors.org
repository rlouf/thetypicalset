#+title: BNN priors

The [[https://arxiv.org/abs/1711.10925][Deep Image Prior]] paper shows that $\mathcal{N}(0, \sigma)$ on the weights + [[id:367b34fb-98ad-44d8-a709-230529083e5b][CNN architecture]] gives a good prior in functional space.

[[https://arxiv.org/abs/2112.01388][Residual Pathway Priors for Soft Equivariance Constraints]] shows a method to add inductive bias to BNNs with CNN architecture.
