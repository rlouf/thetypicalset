Historical perspectives make a lot of sense in economics

Economical systems tend to become more complex as we remove constraints, at the beginning they are fairly simple and we may be able to build models. Then we can move forward in time when new regulation shows up, show data before/after & try to model, etc, etc. It would make a great textbook, and it would inform policy a lot better than the current mess.
